SMARTSMOKER

THE PROBLEM

During my work as an international yachts supplier I had to keep track of my journeys in order to fill the details into an Microsoft excel sheet when back home. Afterwards this file was used by the company to calculate the worked hours and pay the employees accordingly. This process is something i wanted to simplify so that drivers could log their journeys while on the job. After completing a delivery the idea was that a driver could sent the data to administration with a click of the button.
